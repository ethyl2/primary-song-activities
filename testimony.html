1. The witness of the Holy Ghost, As borne by those who know, Has lifted me again to thee, O Father of my soul. 2. I know that thou art in the heav'n. I know the Savior reigns. I know a prophet speaks to us For our eternal gain. 3. My eyes are wet; my heart is full. The Spirit speaks today. O Lord, wilt thou my life renew And in my bosom stay. 4. As testimony fills my heart, It dulls the pain of days. For one brief moment, heaven's view Appears before my gaze.
