What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is a group of words used by designers to fill a space that will eventually contain words that make sense. The Lorem Ipsum words act as a temporary placeholder. Traditionally, they don't make sense and typically are a mixture of Latin and and made-up words.
