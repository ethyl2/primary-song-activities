Show Your Love for Another with a 'Heart Attack'

Here's what you do:

  1. Cut out a bunch of different-sized hearts out of colored paper. You can freehand them, or trace heart-shaped cookie cutters. I like folding my paper in half and then cutting a heart half.
  2. Decorate them if you'd like, with drawings and/or stickers.
  3. Write messages on them. See the printable below for ideas.
  4. Use the hearts to decorate someone's door, desk, or other area. Use painter's tape to attach them, so you don't do any damage to surfaces.
