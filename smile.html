Smiles

Click on the face to change the frown to a smile, and the smile to a frown.

Original lyrics:

If you chance to meet a frown,

Do not let it stay.

Quickly turn it upside down

And smile that frown away.

No one likes a frowning face,

Change it for a smile.

Make the world a better place

By smiling all the while.

Note: I think that it's fine to be sad and feel frowny for a while. When you feel sad but then feel ready to try and feel more positive, hopefully this song can help.

My suggested lyrics:

If you chance to meet a frown,

Know that it's okay.

When ready, turn it upside down

And smile that frown away.

At times you might be feeling sad,

And your face will frown.

Other times you might be glad

And spread the smiles around.
